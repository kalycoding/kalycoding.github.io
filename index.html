Frequently Asked Questions

How does Quran Unlock work?

Simply set your daily Quran reading goal and select which apps you'd like to block. Once you complete your reading, all blocked apps will be unlocked for the day.

How do I set up app blocking?

When you first open the app, you'll be prompted to grant Screen Time permissions. After that, go to Settings in the app and select which apps you want to block.

What if I have an emergency?

Essential apps like Phone, Messages, and emergency services are never blocked. Your safety always comes first.

Can I change my reading goal?

Absolutely! You can adjust your daily reading duration or page count anytime in the app's Settings section.

Does the app track which apps I use?

No. While we use Screen Time API to block apps, we never collect, store, or transmit information about your app usage. Your privacy is fully protected.

How do I reset my streak?

If you'd like to reset your reading streak, go to Settings ‚Üí Statistics ‚Üí Reset Streak. Note that this action cannot be undone.

Troubleshooting

Apps aren't blocking

Make sure you've granted Screen Time permissions in iOS Settings ‚Üí Screen Time ‚Üí Quran Unlock. You may need to restart your device after granting permissions.

Reading progress not saving

Ensure you complete the full reading session without closing the app. If the problem persists, try reinstalling the app or contact our support team.

App crashes or freezes

Please email us with your device model, iOS version, and a description of what you were doing when the crash occurred. We'll investigate and fix it promptly.
